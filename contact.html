Good Weather

Good Weather is a weather hazard forecasting app for tourists. This project was done as a group assignment for IN4MATX 134: Project in User Interaction Software, with 3 other teammates. We were given the task to design software with the purpose to tackle the effects of climate change. Since the class encouraged each team of students to effectively create their own start-up company, our project design process involves the consideration of business segments such as monetization opportunities.

Problem

Climate change brings extreme weather hazards like wildfires, hurricanes, thunderstorms, and more. These hazards not only affect residents in local areas but also impact touristsâ€™ travel schedules. Tourists are usually new to the areas and are not aware of how to navigate such hazards. Yet, there are no useful tools to help tourists plan their trip in conjunction with weather hazards.
